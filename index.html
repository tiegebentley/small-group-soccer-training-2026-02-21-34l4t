Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training program

We offer training programs for players ages 8 through adult. Our coaching approach is tailored to each age group's developmental stage, ensuring that younger players build fundamental skills while older players focus on tactical awareness and competitive play. We assess each player individually to place them in the appropriate group based on skill level rather than age alone, ensuring everyone gets the right level of challenge.

Our small group training sessions consist of 4-8 players per group, with an average of 6 players. This size is intentional—it's large enough to simulate game situations and provide competitive intensity, but small enough that each player receives individual attention and personalized feedback. This ratio ensures our coaches can monitor every player's technique and progress throughout the session while maintaining the high-intensity environment that drives improvement.

We create a supportive, non-judgmental environment where you can learn at your own pace without feeling intimidated. Our coaches are experienced in teaching beginners and will scale the intensity and complexity of drills to match your current ability level. Many of our most successful players started as complete beginners.

We offer flexible pricing options to fit different budgets and schedules. Most players train 1-2 sessions per week, with session packages ranging from drop-in rates to monthly and seasonal commitments. We believe in flexibility, so there's no long-term contract required. For specific pricing details tailored to your needs and frequency preferences, please contact us directly at marcus@smallgroupsoccer.com or visit our website at smallgroupsoccer.com.
