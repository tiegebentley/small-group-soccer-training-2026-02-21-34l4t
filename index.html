About Us

Dedicated to developing the next generation of soccer players in Raleigh and Wake County

Our Story

Small Group Soccer was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, personalized soccer training to players of all levels in Raleigh and Wake County. Our founder, Marcus, recognized a gap in the market for training that combines professional coaching methods with a supportive, community-focused environment. Starting with just a handful of dedicated players, we've grown into a thriving program that has helped hundreds of young athletes reach their soccer goals while building lasting friendships and memories.

Our Mission & Vision

We believe that every player has the potential to improve, and our mission is to unlock that potential through expert coaching, detailed feedback, and an intense training environment. We're committed to developing not just better soccer players, but confident, resilient individuals who understand the value of hard work, teamwork, and perseverance. Our vision is to be the premier small group soccer training program in North Carolina, known for producing technically skilled, tactically intelligent, and mentally tough players who excel both on and off the field.
